DAKSHI MOVEMENT

Disclosing the Ancient Knowledge Systems and Heritage of India

About

Dakshi is a movement that seeks to re-awaken the ancient waiver knowledge system of India. Through this movement, we strive to bring ancient knowledge to the modern world. This includes the study of ancient texts and practices, engaging in discussion and debate, conducting research, hosting lectures, participating in competitions and workshops, and more. By doing so, the movement hopes to make ancient knowledge more accessible to everyone and to bring it to the forefront of today's society. Thats why the motto of this researched based organisation is also "संस्कृति ज्ञान की" which means the culture of Knowledge.

Meaning

Meaning of the word Dakshi is derived from the Sanskrit word Daksh which means Strength or Competent. So through Dakshi we would give strength to Ancient Knowledge in reviving itself.

Established

Established on 19th of December, 2022 on the auspicious occasion of Mokshada Ekadashi, Margashirsha Maas, Vikram Samvat 2079 and Every year Mokshada Ekadashi will be celebrated as the anniversary of Dakshi.

Motto

Motto of Dakshi is Culture of Knowledge or to say "संस्कृति ज्ञान की" ।

Annual Event

The Annual Event is organised on the Very first day of the Month of Chaitra of Hindu Calendar every year On the first anniversary of DAKSI, it was officially renamed DAKSHI - "Disclosing the Ancient Knowledge Systems and Heritage of India"

Dakshi's Overview

VISION

"ॐ न हि ज्ञानेन सदृशंपवित्रमिह विद्यत।।े इस संसारमेंज्ञानके समान पवित्र (अन्य) कुछ भी नहीं है।"

Our Vision is very clear that we want to re-establish the Ancient Indian Culture of Knowledge through the Re-Discovery of Ancient Indian knowledge systems via research and development of the Scriptures. Our Vision is to make Dakshi a platform where anyone can come to know about the true essence of culture and knowledge of Traditions, Rituals, Practices with the scientific Temperament.

MISSION

समयस्य पारंपरिका ज्ञानसम्बंधः

The movement strives to bridge the gap between current and ancient wisdom for a better understanding of the human condition and the world in which we live. And its main aim is to connect cultural antiquity with modernity. We want to make ancient Indian knowledge more reliable or accessible for the present as well as future generations through this movement.

OBJECTIVE

तार्किक एवं सांस्कृतिक समृद्धि

We aim to promote both analytical and critical thinking, as well as their application to our modern environment. As a result, society would better understand its past and be able to determine its future, according to the movement. Our objective is to promote or conserve culture through logic, that is if we follow something we should have the knowledge regarding the same and the Logics in favour of the thing which we follow or spread.

VISITS

सांस्कृतिक दर्शनं

Dakshi members explore culturally significant sites, delving into the Science and Technology behind India's remarkable architecture. Cultural tourism not only enriches local heritage but also serves as a crucial global revenue source. These visits contribute to the development of national culture, with notable locations like Chhatarpur Temple in Delhi, the Iron Pillar of Qutub Complex, and Bateshwar Group of Temples in Madhya Pradesh being explored. These visits are essential for firsthand experiences and understanding the truth at Ground Zero.

RESEARCH WORK

बुद्धिमयं समर्पणं

Dakshi thrives on intellectual research, focusing on an intellectual approach to our movement. Our members conduct ongoing research on diverse topics, presenting their findings through articles and papers. Rooted in deep research and logical reasoning, our commitment to the Indian culture of knowledge is evident in works such as a paper on the Calendrical History of India and articles covering topics like a book review of Al Basham's "The Wonder That Was India" and the concept of motherhood in ancient scriptures. The magazine reflects our members' extensive research, aiming to propagate Bharat's rich culture of knowledge from ancient times.
